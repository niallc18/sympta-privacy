Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Sympta ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal health tracking application that helps you log symptoms, meals, medications, mood, and sleep. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal health information.

Information We Collect

  • Health Data: Symptoms, severity levels, meals, medications, mood, sleep duration, and notes you enter
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve app performance
  • Subscription Data: Purchase information processed by Apple

How Your Data is Stored

  • All your health entries are stored locally on your device
  • If you enable iCloud, your data syncs securely across your Apple devices using your personal iCloud account
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • We do not store your health data on our servers

AI Voice Feature

When you use the Voice + AI feature:

  • Your voice transcript is sent to our secure cloud function
  • The transcript is forwarded to Google's Gemini AI to extract structured health data
  • We do not store your transcripts or AI outputs on our servers
  • We only maintain an anonymous daily counter to enforce usage limits
  • Voice data is processed in real-time and not retained

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties.

  • Your data remains on your device and iCloud account
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not share data with healthcare providers unless you export it yourself

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Download all your data as CSV or PDF at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual entries or all data by deleting the app
  • Access: View all your stored information within the app

Security

  • Data is encrypted on your device using iOS security features
  • iCloud sync uses Apple's end-to-end encryption
  • Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection for app access
  • We follow industry best practices for data protection

Children's Privacy

Sympta is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app. Continued use of Sympta after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
