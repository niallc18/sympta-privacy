FAQ

Can I track symptoms, mood, meals, and sleep together?

Yes. Sympta lets you log symptoms, mood, meals, sleep, and notes in a single daily check‑in.

How does Voice + AI work?

You speak your check‑in naturally. Sympta drafts a structured entry, and you review and edit before saving.

Can I enter symptoms manually?

Yes. Manual entry lets you add symptoms, meals, mood, sleep, and notes with full control.

Does my data stay on my phone?

Your entries stay on your device by default, and you can export or delete your data anytime.

Can I reuse a previous day?

Yes. Reuse a past entry as a template, then adjust anything that changed.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export summaries to PDF (and CSV where supported) to share or keep for your records.

Is Sympta available on iPhone and iPad?

Yes. Sympta is built for iOS and works on both iPhone and iPad.
